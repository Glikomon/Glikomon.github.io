Cooking Steps

Step 1 of 6

We take the kefir and egg out of the refrigerator and let them warm up. In a deep container, break the eggs and mix with sugar and salt.

Step 2 of 6

Preheat the kefir (on the stove or in the microwave) until it becomes warm, but not hot, and pour into the egg mixture.

Step 3 of 6

Sift the flour and pour it into the kefir mixture a little at a time, stirring constantly. It is most convenient to use a whisk. The dough should be thick and without lumps. If desired, you can add soda at this stage — the dough will turn out even more lush.

Step 4 of 6

Preheat the pan, pour a few tablespoons of oil on it, let it warm up and spread a tablespoon of dough. Fry over medium heat until golden brown.

Step 5 of 6

Then turn over and fry on the other side.

Step 6 of 6

Serve with honey, sour cream or condensed milk
